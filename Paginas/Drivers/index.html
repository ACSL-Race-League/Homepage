ACSL Drivers

To elevate the racing standards within ACSL and ensure maximum sportsmanship, we utilize an advanced rating system directly integrated with our race results. This system analyzes every action on track, translating performance and conduct into a personal ACSL Skill Rating (AR) and Safety Rating (SR).

Every driver begins with a baseline of 2.00 SR and 1000 AR. Your standing as a competitor is now defined entirely by your consistency and professionalism during official events. In the near future, these ratings will serve as the benchmark for entry into our most exclusive events, where a minimum Safety Rating will be mandatory to guarantee the integrity and quality of the competition.
