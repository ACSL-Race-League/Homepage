About

ACSL Race League is a professional sim racing league focused on quality, fair play, and competitive racing. The league provides a structured and reliable environment where drivers of all skill levels can take part in well-organized championships. All races are held in Assetto Corsa. An official race takes place every Saturday evening, with a strong focus on realistic driving, clear regulations, and consistent race management. This ensures a fair and respectful competition for all participants.
ACSL Race League currently features three categories: Rookie, Pro, and Endurance. This structure allows drivers to compete at their own level while developing their skills within the league. ACSL Race League is more than just racing. It is a dedicated community of motivated sim racers who challenge each other, aim to improve, and work together to build a professional sim racing league where quality and enjoyment go hand in hand.
